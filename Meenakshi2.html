✦ Our Story

About SRI Meenakshi
Collections

Founded with a passion for fashion and a love for elegant Indian wear, Meenakshi Collections has been dressing families across generations. We believe that every person deserves to look and feel beautiful — whether it's a grand celebration or a quiet weekend.

From rich bridal sarees to breezy everyday salwars, handsome men's kurtas to adorable kids' festive wear — our collection is a celebration of culture, color, and craftsmanship. We also offer premium maggam work, stitching and dry wash services.
